Closer

Individual Project

With the opening of the second child policy, more
and moremothers are facing postpartum depression.
Closer aims to strengthen the interaction between
couples and use the power of family to protect
mothers from depression.

Today, "postpartum depression" and "widowed parenting" are in the news too often to be ignored.

Women are not only expected to take on the responsibilites of the traditional household structure, they are also expected to build their careers. This puts the pressure on young women today at an unprecedented level.

After working women leave their careers, they will have a strong sense of loss and disparity. Depression is very high. Closer treats themental health of new mothers in adifferent way, by creating a middleman, a buffer zone.

Primary Research：Chinese Mothers are suffering

The public mental health insight report, which collected data from 2106 mothers, found that postpartum depression is common among mothers.

Primary Research：The absense of father and husband

In this survey, most mothers mentioned a very important issue. Their husbands did not understand their needs very well, and there was room for improvement in educating taking care of their children.

Interviews Summary

Interview Insight #1

Physical: Accompaning and Spending more time with Baby

Fathers spend little time with their children and mothers mainly take care of babies' daily lives. Fathers are less involved in education, and mothers are the main educators.

Interview Insight #2

Physical: Wife lacks husband's companionship and takes on heavy housework and parenting

In the absence of her husband, her mother took on a lot of physical work, and the task of grocery shopping, cooking, laundry and playing with the children was given to her.

Interview Insight #3

Psychologically: mothers are prone to depression after facing their children alone for long periods of time

In the closed space of the family, mothers often have to face crying children, clean up the messy home, and face all this alone, mothers tend to feel stressed and cause depression..

Interview Insight #4

Psychologically: The mother's bad feelings will be vented on the husband, further intensifying family conflicts

After a busy day, the husband will feel that his wife does not understand him when he faces her accusations. At the same time, the wife feels that the burden of taking care of the children is all on her, the husband is not responsible enough, and the family atmosphere is tense

Analysis of Users' Needs

Need #1

Wife: Regular daily care

Eg. Phone Calls and Messages from husband concerning wife and baby's condition

Need #2

Wife: Understanding and Support from husband

Eg. A simple sentence acknowledging the hard work of the wife and praising her for doing so much for the children and the family

Need #3

Wife: Motivating the husband to contribute more to the family

Eg. Do more housework and undertake children's education

Need #4

Husband: Wife will be able to communicate in a more calm condition

Eg. Have a heart-to-heart communication not shouting and blaming each other.

Need #5

Husband: Wife understands how difficult it is for him in the workplace supporting family

Eg. Not just blaming him for not doing housework, but acknowledge that his effort for bringing family a better life

Need #6

Husband: A Reminder for him to contact his wife

Eg. Husband sometimes just forget to message and care for his wife and baby. A reminder can remind him doing that.
